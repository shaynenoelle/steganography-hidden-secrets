Who is Hidden Secrets?

Hidden Secrets is a free, open-source web application for digital watermarking and steganography. It allows you to embed hidden messages into images and extract them later - all within your browser, with no server involvement.

We believe in making cryptography and steganography accessible to everyone. Whether you're an artist protecting your work, a researcher exploring steganography, or someone curious about digital security, this tool is for you.

In an increasingly digital world, the ability to protect your creative work and maintain privacy is essential. Hidden Secrets empowers you to add an extra layer of security to your images, embedding copyright information, authentication markers, or secret messages that only you can retrieve.

Our Core Values

Privacy & Security

Your data belongs to you. All processing happens locally in your browser - we never store, collect, or transmit your images or watermarks to any server.

Open Source

Transparency is key. Our code is open for everyone to inspect, learn from, and contribute to. We believe in the power of collaborative development.

Accessibility

Technology should be for everyone. We design with accessibility in mind, ensuring our tools are usable by people of all skill levels and abilities.

Innovation

We constantly explore new ways to improve digital security and privacy, pushing the boundaries of what's possible with browser-based cryptography.
